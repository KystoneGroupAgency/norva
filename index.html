Common questions

The questions teams ask before they commit.

How long until we see the first result?

We design every engagement around an early proof of value, so you see something tangible quickly — usually within the first weeks — before any larger commitment.

Do you replace our team or work alongside it?

Either. We can own the full data journey, co-build with your people, or plug in as specialists to reinforce the team you already have.

How do you handle the security of our data?

Governance comes first: LGPD-aligned handling, row-level security, isolated environments, documented models, and full traceability of who accessed what.

Will we be locked into a specific tool?

No. We're tool-agnostic and integrate with what you already use. We recommend the right technology for your context — not whatever is trendy.

We already have dashboards. Can you still help?

Often that's where we add the most value — auditing what exists, fixing trust in the data, and turning reports people ignore into decisions they rely on.

How does contracting work?

After the diagnosis we present a clear proposal with defined scope, timeline, and price. You take the time you need to compare and align internally — no pressure.

What size and sector of company do you serve?

We work across commercial, financial, marketing, and industrial operations — from teams taking their first structured step to organizations scaling a mature data practice.

What happens after delivery?

We don't disappear. We measure adoption, support your team, and evolve the solution as your business changes — that's the whole point.
