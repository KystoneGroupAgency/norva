Why a portal

Great dashboards no one opens — and a licensing bill that keeps climbing.

Two problems quietly cap the value of BI in most companies. Intelligence Portal was built to remove both.

01

Per-seat licensing doesn't scale

Every viewer needs a paid Power BI seat. Roll BI out company-wide and the bill grows with every new hire — so access gets rationed to a few, and the data stays locked away from the people who need it.

02

Dashboards show, they don't tell

People open a report, stare at the charts, and still ask "so what actually changed?" Reading the data is left entirely to the viewer — and most never get past the visual to the decision.
